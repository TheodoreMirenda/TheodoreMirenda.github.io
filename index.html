Hey! I'm Theodore Mirenda a Unity Developer located in Eastern Tennessee. I graduated in '19 with a BS in Cybersecurity but after a few years in the industry, I moved on to the more fullfilling career of game dev.

Since the pivot, I've worked fulltime as a mobile Game Developer along with contract positions for Web 3 gaming studios, all while building a YouTube Channel focused around Unity and Game Development.

I love developing video games, to the point where often I feel more enjoyment working on games than playing them. Feel free to contact me here.
