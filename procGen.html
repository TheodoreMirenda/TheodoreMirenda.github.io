Project Overview

I wanted to create a cave system, but due to caves being irregular in nature, I had to find a way to procedurally generate an irregular grid. This was acomplished by writing a complex algorithm to generate an irregular layout of quadralaterals, each having 4 sides. I was able to manipulate prebuilt meshes to squish them into them into the irregular shapes, but with this constraint on the amount of sides, it allowed me to use a wave function collapse algorithm as a ruleset to generate connectivity between the quads.
